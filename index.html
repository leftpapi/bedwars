How to play

Guess the mystery BedWars kit. After each guess, every column lights up:

  • GOLD — exact match
  • SILVER — close (length is off by ≤2, or arrow shows higher/lower)
  • STONE — no match

Length and Words show an arrow (↑/↓) telling you if the answer's value is higher or lower than your guess.

Daily mode gives everyone the same kit each day, resetting at midnight. Unlimited mode is a random kit every time — play as much as you want.
